Privacy Policy

Training Dashboard · Last updated: 2026

Overview

This is a personal training dashboard built for private use. It connects to WHOOP and Strava to display health and activity data in one place, with AI-generated coaching insights.

Data We Access

When you connect your accounts, this app reads the following data from WHOOP and Strava via their official APIs:

WHOOP: Recovery scores, HRV, resting heart rate, sleep data, strain, and cycle data.

Strava: Your recent activity list including distance, pace, heart rate, and activity type.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to display your personal dashboard and to generate AI coaching insights via the Anthropic Claude API. The AI receives a summary of your metrics (no personal identifiers) to produce training recommendations.

Data Storage

This app does not operate a server or database. No data is stored anywhere outside your own browser. Access tokens from WHOOP and Strava are stored in your browser's session storage only, and are cleared when you close the tab or disconnect.

Third-Party Services

This app communicates with the following third-party services:

WHOOP API — to read your health data. Governed by WHOOP's Privacy Policy.

Strava API — to read your activity data. Governed by Strava's Privacy Policy.

Anthropic Claude API — to generate AI coaching insights. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. The only external communication is directly with the three services listed above.

Your Rights

You can revoke this app's access to your WHOOP or Strava account at any time through their respective account settings pages. Disconnecting in the dashboard clears all locally stored tokens immediately.

Contact

This is a personal project. If you have questions about how your data is handled, you can reach out via your GitHub repository.
